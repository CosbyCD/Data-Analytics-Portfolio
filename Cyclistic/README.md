# Cyclistic Bike-Share Marketing Analysis
Cyclistic Bike-Share Marketing Conversion Project
Google Data Analytics Certificate — Coursera Capstone

## Overview
Cyclistic is a prominent bike-share program in Chicago with a fleet of 5,824 bikes stationed across 692 docking points. With an array of bike categories, styles, and flexible pricing plans, Cyclistic serves a diverse spectrum of riders — from leisurely cyclists to daily commuters.
This project analyzes extensive historical bike trip data to understand the behavioral differences between casual riders and annual members, and to develop a data-driven marketing strategy focused on converting casual riders into long-term members.

## Analytical Questions

How do annual members and casual riders engage with Cyclistic bikes in distinctive ways?
What notable distinctions exist in usage patterns between these two rider segments?
What factors prompt casual riders to contemplate the shift toward annual membership?
How can digital media platforms contribute to converting casual riders into dedicated long-term members?
